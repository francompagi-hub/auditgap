The Shift

Search behaviour changed. Most stores don't know it yet.

A buyer looking for 'best running shoes for flat feet' used to see 10 blue links. Now they see an AI-generated answer recommending three brands by name — with no link to your store, even if you rank on page one of Google. The buyer clicks one of those three names. You were never visible.

This is not a future problem. AI-assisted search queries now account for a growing share of product discovery in e-commerce. Stores appearing in AI-generated answers receive disproportionate click share because AI answers present as recommendations, not just results. The stores not appearing are losing market share to competitors who optimised for AI indexability.

Why AI systems ignore well-optimised stores:

AI answer systems index your content differently from search crawlers. Structured data, schema markup, content format, authority signals, and brand mention patterns all influence whether an AI system surfaces your store in answers. A store with excellent Google rankings may have zero AI visibility — and vice versa. These are different systems with different inputs.

The Assessment

What the audit covers

We test your store's visibility across the four primary AI answer platforms and diagnose the specific technical and content barriers preventing you from appearing as a recommended result.

  • Visibility testing across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overview

    Live query testing across multiple product categories and buyer intent phrases. We document where you appear, where you don't, and which competitors are being surfaced instead.

  • Brand mention analysis in AI-generated answers

    How often is your brand mentioned in AI responses to category queries? We benchmark this against direct competitors and identify the gap.

  • Schema markup and structured data assessment

    Product schema, organisation schema, FAQ schema, and review schema all influence AI indexability. We audit what you have implemented, what is missing, and what is incorrectly formatted.

  • Content structure analysis for AI indexability

    AI systems prefer clearly structured, authoritative, factually dense content. We evaluate whether your product pages, category pages, and supporting content are formatted to be AI-readable.

  • Competitor presence comparison

    A direct side-by-side audit of where your competitors appear in AI answers across the same queries. This identifies the specific optimisations they have made that you have not.
